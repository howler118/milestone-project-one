From the vault

Each week we roll back the years and take a look at some classic moments from the past. This week we take a look at the Woods cup final in the 2011-12 season...

Having already won the Division 2 title and Paul Henry cup, the Millers would need to beat a strong Douglas Royals side to secure a historic treble. The stage was set, the weather was perfect, all the players fired up a ready to make history. In life, things don't always go to plan and the Millers found themselves 1-0 down and chasing the game. With the clock ticking and the chance for a treble slipping away, AJ Suckling rose high to meet a superb cross after a sweet passing move down the left hand side. This equaliser galvanised the Millers who would eventually go on to win the game 3-1 and secure the historic treble and complete the Millers most successful season.

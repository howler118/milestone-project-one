How to find us

Union Mills FC grounds are located in the heart of Braddan parish, just on the outskirts of Union Mills village. From Quarterbridge, follow the A1 west and turn right at the roundabout at Braddan bridge. Pass Braddan school on the right and turn left at the first roundabout. Turn left at the second round about and pass Nobles Hospital on the right. When you get to Stang stores roundabout, turn right and head down the road past a couple of houses until there is a dip in the road and the road widens and you should see the gates for UMFC.
